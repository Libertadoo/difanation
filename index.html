Welcome to the official website of Portuguese fashion photographer Daniel Filipe. Through his career he has worked for household model-agencies like "ELITE", "ONWAY" and "KARACTER". Daniel's devotion, hard-work and vivid imagination always result in stunning and engaging shots for you and your fashion business.

About me

As an artist I express myself through my work, thus always searching for a creative and unusual approach. In the hum of city madness, life in the fast lane and forever changing fashion trends, I aim to tell stories through my photographs. Capturing souls, and not just bodies is my ultimate priority as a fashion photographer from a new generation.

It all started when I was very little, running around the house, taking pictures of my parents. and eversince it has been my passion and my obsession - photographing not just anything, but especially people. There just seems to be something fascinating in their eyes, their curves, their radiance. And when I capture this tiny pea of magic in my photographs, I call the day a total success.
